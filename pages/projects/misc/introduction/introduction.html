Introduction to Computer Club


Hello, fellow computer club member. Welcome to the computer club!
In this year's computer club, we'll be teaching 3 things:

  • 2 Programming languages (Java, C++)
  • UX design skills
  • Ethical Hacking

    • Programming

    The main reason why we choose to teach Java, and C++ is that they are high-level languages and are easier to understand and have more general purposes. We will teach you the basics and syntax of those languages. We will also do a few projects with them. For example: Random number generator, Calculator app, Number guessing game, etc.

    UX Designing

    Designing an eye-catching website or application is essential to capturing people's interest in them. It may look simple, but it is certainly more complex than you would think. We will teach you what software to use and how to make it more attractive.

    Ethical Hacking

    With cybercriminals being more active than ever at hacking others. Having a good understanding of some of their hacking methods can help defend them a.k.a Network threats. So in the ethical hacking course, we'll teach you how to test your home networks to see if they're vulnerable to those attacks and how to secure them. Which includes Port scanning, Packets grabbing and Penetration testing.

    Others

    We'll also provide you with each programming lesson's source code, along with full documentation and tutorials. We also provide a page dedicated to those who want to challenge themselves in programming or cybersecurity with harder objectives than those during the meetings and will NOT provide source code. But we will provide hints on how to do them.
    That's all we have to say here. We hope you will have a great time learning here and hope to see you at the next computer club meeting.
